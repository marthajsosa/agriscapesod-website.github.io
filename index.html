FAQ

Good questions, straight answers.

How much does sod cost in Florida?

It depends on square footage and grass variety. Use the instant estimate above for a ballpark, then request a free quote for exact, confirmed pricing — including free local delivery.

Do you deliver and install, or just supply?

Both. We grow, deliver, and install — including old turf removal and ground prep — or deliver fresh-cut sod by the pallet or truckload.

How fresh is the sod?

It's cut to order and harvested within 24 hours of delivery, so it arrives fresh and ready to root.

Do you handle large commercial and government projects?

Yes — we have the fleet and crews to serve HOAs, developers, contractors, and public infrastructure projects across Florida. See our Commercial & Government page for capacity details.

What areas do you serve?

Residential service covers Sarasota–Bradenton–Venice and Fort Myers–Cape Coral. Commercial and government projects extend to Tampa–St. Pete, Orlando, and the wider Southwest Florida region.
